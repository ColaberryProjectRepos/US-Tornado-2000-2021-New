Total Fatalities Contribution Decomposition Tree 22


Steps
  • Click on an empty space on the canvas, and from the visualizations pane select Decomposition Tree
  • Create Region Hierarchy in US_States_Region table by  clicking on three dots near Region field and give Create Hierarchy
  • Now click on three dots near State field and give Add to Hierarchy and give Region Hierarchy
Adding Data to the Visual
  • Drag and drop Fatalities to the Analyze field
  • Drag and drop the Region Hierarchy-Region,State  to the Explain by field
  • Tooltips = Count,Fatality,Injury,Length,Width,YoY Count,YoY Fatality,YoY Injury,YoY Length,YoY Width
  • Cross Report-On
