1.Tornado Count Year over Year


YOY Count  
  •  Click on an empty space on the canvas, and from the visualizations pane select Card.
  •   From  ‘US_Tornado_2000_2021’table, drag YoY Count into the Fields section.
  •  Fill up figures provided below for the necessary fields.
  • General: Properties, Size, 
  •  Height=24, 
  •  Width=57, 
  •   Position, 
  •   Horizontal=128
  •   Vertical=204
  •   Title=Off,
  •   Effects, Background =On, Color=#E6E6E6,Transparency=16%, Visual border=off
  •   Visual: Callout Value, Font=Arial (11) Bold, Font color= #12239E, Category label=Off
  • Display Units-Auto, Value decimal places-Auto
  • Category Label-Off
