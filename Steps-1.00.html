Problem Statement 1




Understanding and mitigating risks associated with US tornadoes is essential due to their potential for damaging infrastructure, endangering lives, and disrupting communities. From 2000 to 2021, the United States experienced a total of 26,000 tornado occurrences. The year 2004 recorded the highest count with 1,817 tornadoes,  Fatalities resulting from tornado totaled 1,649, with the highest fatality count of 553 in 2011. In 2008, the tornado count increased by 54% compared to 2007. In 2011, there was a staggering 1,129% increase in the fatality rate compared to 2010, and a 682% increase in the injury rate during the same period.
