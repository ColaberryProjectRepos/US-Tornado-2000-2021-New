Transforming the data in power Query Editor 9

In this step you will clean and transform the data

1.Remove 'slat','slon','elat','elon' columns that are not needed for the report

In the Home ribbon, go to Transform and right click on ‘slat’,'slon','elat' and 'elon' columns and select remove

Follow the gif step by step



2.Filtering the Year for 2000 to 2021 
  • Filter 'yr' column to filter the years from  2000 to 2021.
  • Right click ‘yr’ column and click on Number filters and give greater than or equal to 2000 and then ok. 
  • After filtering the year remove 'yr','mo','dy' as they are already in the date column.
Follow the gif step by step



3.Filter the 'mag' column and select the records with magnitude 0 to 5 and remove any null or blank value in magnitude

4.Rename the columns 'st' to 'State','mag' to 'Magnitude(EF Scale),'inj' to 'Injuries','fat' to 'Fatalities','len' to 'Length' and 'wid' to 'Width'

5.Remove any null or blank values in State column of the records

Follow the gif step by step



6.Change the name of the table to 'US_Tornado_2000_2021'

  • Right click on 'us_tornado_dataset_1950_2021' and click on rename and give name as 'US_Tornado_2000_2021'
  • Click on file and select close and apply.
